kese

1. bath glove made of coarse cloth (used in a Turkish bath for rubbing the skin). 2. small cloth bag; moneybag, purse. 3. (one´s) financial resources. 4. marsupium, pouch. 5. sac, cyst, bursa. ––nin ağzını açmak to begin to spend money freely. ––sine (hiç) bir şey girmemek not to benefit at all. ––nin dibi görünmek to run out of money. ––sine güvenmek to know that one can afford to buy something. –– sürmek /a/ to rub (someone) with a kese.
