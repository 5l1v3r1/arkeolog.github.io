,-kı 1. certain, sure. 2. certainly, undoubtedly.

1. /a/ contrary to, in violation of; opposed to, against. 2. opposing; of the opposition, opposition. 3. contrary, adverse. 4. opponent. 5. conflicting, incompatible.

1. maker or seller of milk puddings. 2. store selling milk puddings, milk bar. 3. colloq. namby-pamby, mollycoddled.

accountant; bookkeeper.
