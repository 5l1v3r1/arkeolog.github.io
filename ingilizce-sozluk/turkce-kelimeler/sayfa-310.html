Arama

komşu

1. neighbor. 2. neighboring, adjacent. –– açılar geom. adjacent angles. –– kapısına çevirmek /ı/ to act as if (a far-off place) were nearby. –– komşunun külüne/tütününe muhtaçtır. proverb Even in the smallest of matters one neighbor can help another. –– olmak to become neighbors. ––da pişer, bize de düşer. colloq. Maybe we´ll benefit from our neighbor´s good fortune. ––nun tavuğu komşuya kaz görünür. proverb The grass is always greener on the other side of the fence.

komşuluk

1. being a neighbor. 2. neighborliness. –– etmek (for neighbors) to be friendly with each other.

kömür

1. coal. 2. charcoal. 3. coal-black. –– çarpmak /ı/ for charcoal fumes to give (someone) a headache. –– gibi coal-black. –– gözlü (person) with coal-black eyes. –– işçisi coal miner, Brit. collier. –– kalem charcoal pencil. –– katranı coal tar. –– ocağı coal mine.

kömürcü

1. coal dealer. 2. stoker. 3. charcoal burner. –– çırağına dönmek (for someone) to get black (with grime).

kömürcülük

1. the coal business, the work of a coal dealer. 2. the work of a stoker. 3. charcoal burning, the work of a charcoal burner.

kömürleşmek

1. to turn into coal, coalify. 2. to turn into charcoal.

kömürleştirmek

kömürlük

konakçı

konaklama
