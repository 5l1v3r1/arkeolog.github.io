muaf

/dan/ 1. exempt, excused, free (from). 2. med. immune (to). –– tutmak /ı, dan/ to exempt (someone, something) from.

muafiyet

,-ti 1. exemption. 2. med. immunity.

muaflık

1. exemption. 2. med. immunity.

muahede

treaty.

muallak

,-kı 1. hung, hanging, suspended. 2. undecided, unresolved, up in the air. 3. /a/ dependent on, contingent on. ––ta kalmak/olmak to remain undecided, remain unresolved.

muallim

teacher.

muallime

(female) teacher.

muamele

1. treatment, conduct, behavior. 2. the processing (of a petition or application). 3. doing business with, trading, buying and selling. 4. colloq. sexual intercourse. –– etmek to act, behave; /a/ to treat.

muamma

1. enigma, mystery. 2. riddle (word game).

muammalı

enigmatic, mysterious.

muare

1. moiré, (something) which has a wavy or watery appearance. 2. moiré, wavy or watery appearance.

muarız

1. /a/ opposed (to). 2. opponent.

muaşeret

,-ti social intercourse, social relations. –– adabı etiquette, the rules of conduct.

muasır

1. contemporary, contemporaneous. 2. (a) contemporary.

muavenet

,-ti help, assistance. –– etmek /a/ to help, assist.

muavin

helper, assistant.

muayene

1. inspection, examination. 2. med. examination. –– etmek /ı/ 1. to inspect, examine. 2. med. to examine. –– memuru customs officer.

muayeneci

1. customs officer. 2. inspector, examiner.

muayenehane

doctor's office, consulting room, Brit. surgery.

muayyen

fixed, set, determined; definite.

muazzam

1. great, huge and imposing. 2. colloq. terrific, astounding; magnificent.

muazzep

mentally tormented, suffering. –– etmek /ı/ to make (someone) suffer mental discomfort, torture. –– olmak to suffer mental discomfort, be tortured.

muazzez

held in high regard, cherished, esteemed.

mübadele

exchange; trade, swapping, barter. –– etmek /ı/ to exchange; to trade, swap, barter.

mübadil

1. exchangee; something exchanged. 2. (something, someone) which/who has been exchanged. 3. exchangee (sent to Turkey from Greece, or vice versa, during the population exchange which followed the signing of the Treaty of Lausanne in 1923).

mubah

1. neither enjoined nor forbidden by the Shari´a. 2. colloq. allowed, permissible.

mübalağa

exaggeration. –– etmek /ı/ to exaggerate.

mübalağacı

1. exaggerator. 2. inclined to exaggerate.

mübalağalı

exaggerated, blown-up, overstated.

mübarek

1. blessed, enjoying divine favor; holy, sacred; auspicious; beatific. 2. (something) that gives happiness or prosperity. 3. colloq. blasted, confounded. 4. colloq. the blasted thing; the confounded fellow. M––! colloq. 1. Bless his heart!/Bless its heart! 2. You son of a gun! –– olsun. colloq. May (something, someone) be blessed.: Bayramınız mübarek olsun. May your Bairam be a blessed one. Yeni eviniz mübarek olsun. May your new home be blessed.

mübaşir

court crier, crier (in a court of law).

mubayaa

purchasing, buying. –– etmek /ı/ to purchase, buy.

mubayaacı

1. purchaser, buyer. 2. formerly stockbroker.

mübrem

1. inescapable, ineluctable. 2. indispensable. 3. urgent, pressing.

mücadele

struggle; fight; fray; contention. –– etmek /la/ to fight, struggle, or contend (with).

mücadeleci

1. fighter; contender. 2. (someone) who is willing to fight or struggle; (someone) who relishes a fight. 3. combative, fighting.

mücahit

1. fighter for the Islamic faith. 2. fighter (for a sacred cause or ideal).

mücbir

compelling, (something) that compels. –– sebepler law force majeure, circumstances beyond one´s control.

mücehhez

/la/ 1. equipped (with), furnished (with), fitted out (with); possessing. 2. prepared or ready (with).

mücellit

bookbinder, binder.

mücellithane

bookbindery,bindery.

mücerret

1. abstract (as opposed to concrete). 2. pure, unadulterated. 3. celibate (man). 4. (a) celibate, celibate man.

mücessem

1. personified, incarnate. 2. solid, three-dimensional.

mücevher

piece of jewelry, jewel.

mücevherat

,-tı jewelry, Brit. jewellery.

mücevherci

jeweler, Brit. jeweller.

mucibince

as required by; in accordance with: muahede mucibince in accordance with the treaty.

mucip

1. cause, reason. 2. (something) which necessitates. –– olmak /ı/ to cause, necessitate, entail. –– sebep justification, sufficient reason, good cause.

mucit

1. inventor, discoverer. 2. creative, intentive.

mucize

miracle.

mucizeli

miracle-working, wonder-working, miraculous.

mucizevi

miraculous, miracular.

muço

1. young deckhand; young hand (on a ship). 2. cabin boy. 3. colloq. boy who works in a bar.

mücrim

1. criminal, felon. 2. guilty.

mucur

1. small bits of coal or charcoal. 2. fine gravel (that is mixed with asphalt to form paving). 3. slag, dross, scoria. 4. debris, trash, refuse, rubbish.

mücver

a fried patty the chief ingredient of which is squash.

müdafaa

defense. –– etmek /ı/ to defend. –– hakkı law the right to defend oneself before a court. –– hattı mil. line of defense.

müdafaasız

undefended, defenseless.

müdafi

,-ii 1. defender. 2. law counsel for the defense. 3. soccer back.

müdahale

interference; intervention. –– etmek /a/ to interfere; to intervene.
