salahiyet

,-ti authority, (delegated) power, authorization. –– dairesi the scope or compass of one´s authority. –– vermek /a/ to authorize (someone) (to do something).

salahiyetli

(someone) who has authority; /a/ authorized to, empowered to (do something).

salahiyetsiz

(someone) who lacks authority.

salahiyettar

see salahiyetli.

half-witted, feeble-minded, imbecilic, dunderheaded, addlepated.

salaklaşmak

to begin to act like a half-wit.

salaklık

half-wittedness, dunderheadedness, imbecility.

salami.

salamandra

salamanje

dining room.

salamura

1. brine (used for pickling food). 2. any food which has been pickled in brine. 3. (food) which has been pickled in brine.

salamuralık

1. (food) suitable for pickling in brine. 2. (salt) suitable for making brine.

salangan

salangane (a swift whose nest is edible).

salapurya

a single-masted sailing vessel (once used to transport goods in the waters surrounding Istanbul). –– gibi big, clumsy-looking (shoe).

salaş

1. wooden booth or market stall; temporary wooden shack or shed. 2. temporary, shed-like, wooden (building).

salaşpur

a loosely woven cotton fabric (used for linings).

salat

,-tı Islam 1. ritual prayer, namaz. 2. prayer in which God is asked to bless the Prophet Muhammad.

salata

1. cucumber. 2. (food) which is suitable to use in making a salad. 3. slang aged teacher.

,-tı Islam 1. a prayer in which God is asked to bless the Prophet Muhammad and his descendants. 2. ritual prayers, namaz. –– getirmek/okumak to say the salavat.

salça

1. tomato sauce; tomato paste. 2. gravy, sauce. –– kâsesi gravy boat, sauceboat.

salçalık

saldırgan

aggressive, disposed to attack, belligerent, truculent.

saldırganlık

aggressiveness, belligerence, truculence.

saldırı

attack, assault; aggression; mil. offense. –– silahları mil. offensive weapons. –– teknesi assault boat. ––ya uğramak to be attacked, come under attack.

saldırıcı

aggressive, prone to attack, belligerent, truculent.

saldırma

1. attacking (someone, something). 2. a large knife.

saldırmak

1. /a/ to attack, assail, assault (someone, something); to rush; to charge, charge at; to hurl oneself/itself against/upon. 2. /a/ chem. to act on; to dissolve. 3. /ı, a/ to cause (something, someone) to attack (something, someone).

being a maker or seller of salep (the drink).

salimen

safely, safe and sound.

salıncaklı

used in: –– iskemle/koltuk rocking chair.

salınım

1. to sway; to oscillate. 2. /a/ to be sent to, be dispatched to. 3. /a/ (for an animal) to be put out to pasture in (a place); (for something) to be channeled or directed into (a place). 4. /a/ (for something) to be lowered into (a place). 5. /a/ (for something) to be added to (a food). salına salına yürümek to walk with a swaying movement.

sallamak

sallamamak

/ı/ slang not to care about; to pay no attention to.

sallandırmak
