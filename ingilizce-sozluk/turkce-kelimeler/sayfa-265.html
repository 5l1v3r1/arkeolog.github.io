kala

kalan

kalburlamak

/ı/ to sift, sieve; to screen; to riddle.

kalburlatmak

kaldırım

1. sidewalk, Brit. pavement. 2. stone pavement. 3. horizontal rock strata at a shoreline. –– süpürgesi/yosması colloq. 1. streetwalker, prostitute. 2. gadabout woman, woman who never stays at home. –– çiğnemek to become more knowledgeable or sophisticated by living in the city. –– döşemek to lay down paving stones. –– mühendisi colloq. loafer, idler; sidewalk superintendent. –– taşı paving stone.

/ı,a/ 1. to have (something) raised or lifted. 2. to have (something) removed. 3. to have (something) abolished.
